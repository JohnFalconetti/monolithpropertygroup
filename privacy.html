Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Monolith Property Group ("Monolith," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and how we use it. By using our site or contacting us, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. That includes:

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Property details you share when requesting an offer, such as the address, condition, and your timeline.
  • Investor details you submit when joining our buyers list, such as your target price range, preferred areas, and property types.

We may also collect basic, non-identifying technical information (such as general usage data) that helps the site function. We do not knowingly collect more than we need.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Prepare and send you a cash offer, and respond to your inquiry.
  • Send off-market deal alerts and updates to investors who have joined our buyers list.
  • Communicate with you about your property or your interest in our deals.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our business and website.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for others to market to you. We share it only in limited ways:

  • With service providers who help us run our business, such as our email and newsletter platform, our website host, and our phone and messaging tools. They may process your information only to provide those services to us.
  • When required by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Email and communications

If you join our investor buyers list, you are opting in to receive emails from us, and you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any message. If you contact us as a seller, we will use your details to follow up about your specific inquiry, and we will not add you to the investor list.

Cookies and third-party tools

Monolith does not set advertising or tracking cookies of its own, and we do not use analytics or ad pixels to profile you. The site relies only on cookies that are essential for basic functionality.

Some third-party tools embedded on the site, such as our subscribe form and the web fonts we load, may set their own cookies or receive basic request data, governed by their own privacy policies. You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended if you do.

If we add analytics or advertising tools in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent first.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you may unsubscribe from our emails at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details below, and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

Our website is intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site means you accept the current version.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach us at legal@monolithpropertygroup.com.

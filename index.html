Common questions

Good to know before you reach out.

Is the cash offer really no-obligation?

Yes. We'll give you a number and explain how we got there. If it doesn't work for you, there's no cost and no pressure, and you can walk away any time.

What types of properties do you acquire?

Single-family homes, condominiums, duplexes, and small multi-family, in any condition. Occupied, vacant, inherited, or tenant-occupied. Anywhere on the First Coast, we'll review it.

How fast can you actually close?

Because we pay cash, there's no lender to wait on. We can close in as little as about a week, or later if you need more time to make your next move.

Are there any fees or commissions?

None. There's no agent commission and no listing costs. The offer we agree on is what you walk away with.

Will my offer be as high as a retail listing?

Usually a bit lower. That's the honest trade-off for speed, certainty, and buying as-is with no repairs or fees. For many sellers, that convenience is worth more than chasing the last dollar over several months.
